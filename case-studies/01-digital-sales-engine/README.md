# Digital Sales Engine

**Company:** iTecknologi Group of Companies
**Industry:** Vehicle Tracking & IoT
**Role:** Digital Marketing Manager
**Project Duration:** 2021–2022 (12 Months)

---

## Project Overview

This case study documents how I built a direct digital sales channel for iTecknologi Group of Companies and reduced dependence on dealership partnerships and field sales.

---

## Business Challenge

Before this project, iTecknologi relied heavily on traditional sales channels that were expensive, hard to scale, and limited in visibility. Dealership partnerships involved high commissions and gave very little data. Field sales were constrained by team bandwidth and long sales cycles.

The business needed a measurable, scalable channel that could generate qualified demand directly.

---

## Objectives

* Build a digital sales channel from scratch
* Reduce customer acquisition cost compared with traditional channels
* Improve lead quality and sales efficiency
* Create a repeatable system that could scale without proportional headcount growth
* Use data to guide campaign decisions and sales prioritization

## Implementation

### Phase 1: Pilot

The first six months were used to prove the unit economics of digital acquisition.

I built the conversion infrastructure with a landing page that presented three pricing tiers and added full tracking through GA4 and GTM. I then ran structured Facebook lead generation campaigns and tested creative and headline variations on a monthly basis.

To improve lead quality, I reviewed lead data daily and categorized more than 3,200 leads. One important insight was that social proof, especially the message **“94% stolen vehicle recovery rate,”** performed better than feature-based messaging.

### Phase 2: Scale

Once the pilot proved the model, I expanded the channel.

I diversified the media mix by adding Google Display campaigns, built retargeting funnels for warm leads, and introduced a faster response process through CRM automation and team training. I also documented SOPs so the system could continue running beyond my direct involvement.

## Business Impact

---

## Key Learnings

1. Data earns trust faster than opinion.
2. Social proof outperformed product feature messaging.
3. Lead quality improved when the sales team responded faster.
4. Daily analysis reduced wasted spend and improved qualification.
5. Documentation created continuity beyond the original campaign period.
